Fans can keep up with the BBL live scores in this segment.Perth Scorchers will begin their title defense on December 17, 2022, against the previous year’s finalists Sydney Sixers. All teams will be giving their best to emerge on top of the BBL points table, so as to make their way to the next stage.The group stage of the tournament is scheduled between December 13, 2022, till January 25, 2023. During this period, only December 25 will be an off day owing to Christmas. Notably, on all other days between the aforementioned time period, there will be at least one BBL game happening. The Eliminator is scheduled for January 27, and the Knockout games will be played on January 28 and January 29 respectively.
